Oh hey, these are some notes. They'll be hidden in your presentation, but you can see them if you open the speaker notes window (hit 's' on your keyboard).

Many of you may have expected us to give a polemic talk today. A talk that would argue why and how the text-as-graph model is so far superior to text-as-XML and while at it we would call a spade a spade and show you why graph models are a wiser choice than TEI.

So that indeed is not what we are arguing today.

While exploring alternative models for digital texts we came to understand the digital text in an even more fundamental way than we thought we did. That is what this paper is about.
